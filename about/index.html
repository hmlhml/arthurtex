Welcome to Arthurtex, worldwide leaders in Woven Fabrics.

Arthurtex is based in Azusa, California and is a premier supplier of various woven fabrics, fleece and PVC for a number of markets. The name is new, but our foundation has been in business for over half a century! Our strength lies in our supply chain and production capacity. We have a strong supply chain from Indian, Pakistan and China. We control the entire production process, from fiber to finish, and guarantee the highest quality goods at the most competitive price points in the market!

Arthurtex specializes in all kinds of fabrics. Specifically, Twill, Poplin, Denim,Blanket and PVC. We are also very strong in Fleece!

We produce for a large number of high quality distributors around the globe, so we understand the market’s expectations.

We work closely with our customer base to manufacture, test and certify that all of our products meet their needs and expectations, and are capable of fully developing new or customized products for targeted end uses.

We deliver on time, per your specifications, and at a cost that will allow you to compete as effectively as possible.
